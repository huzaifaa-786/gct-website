Life at GCT Bhakkar

At GCT Bhakkar, we believe education extends beyond the classroom. Our campus provides a nurturing environment where students can grow personally and professionally.

From state-of-the-art laboratories to sprawling sports grounds, from peaceful prayer spaces to vibrant celebration halls — our campus offers everything a student needs for holistic development.

We encourage students to participate in extracurricular activities, sports, and community events that build character, leadership skills, and lasting friendships.
